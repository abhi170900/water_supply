The purpose of supplying water is to provide access to clean and safe
drinking water for individuals and communities. Water is an essential
resource that is needed for survival, and access to clean water is
crucial for maintaining good health and preventing waterborne illnesses.

In addition to providing clean drinking water, water supply systems
also play a crucial role in protecting public health and the
environment. Proper treatment and disposal of wastewater are
critical to prevent the spread of waterborne diseases and pollution.

Overall, the purpose of supplying water is to ensure that
individuals and communities have access to a safe, reliable, and
sustainable water supply for their daily needs.
